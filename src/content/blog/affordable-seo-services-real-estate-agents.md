Most real estate agents rely on Zillow, Realtor.com, and other platforms for leads. The problem? You're paying premium prices for leads that are also sent to your competitors.

SEO helps you build your own lead generation engine—one that brings buyers and sellers directly to you, not through a middleman.

## The Real Estate Search Opportunity

People searching for homes and real estate services use Google constantly:

**High-intent real estate searches:**
- "Homes for sale in [neighborhood/city]"
- "Real estate agent [city name]"
- "[City] housing market 2026"
- "Best neighborhoods in [city] for families"
- "How to sell my house fast [city]"

Ranking for these searches puts you in front of motivated buyers and sellers at the exact moment they need an agent.

## Building Your Online Presence

### Your Website

Your website should be more than a digital business card. It should:

- Showcase your listings and sold properties
- Provide valuable information about your market
- Capture leads through multiple touchpoints
- Build trust through testimonials and credentials

### Content That Attracts Buyers

Buyers research extensively before contacting an agent. Content that answers their questions brings them to you:

- "What to know before buying in [neighborhood]"
- "[City] first-time homebuyer guide"
- "Is [neighborhood] a good investment?"

### Seller-Focused Content

Sellers also search before listing. Capture their attention with:

- "[City] home value estimator"
- "How to prepare your home for sale"
- "Best time to sell in [city]"
